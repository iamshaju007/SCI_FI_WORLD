Gateway to Future
"In the neon-lit shadows of a world drowning in technology, we are all just ghosts in the machine, searching for humanity in a digital wasteland."
It is the mathematical year 3906. Humanity has expanded its reach to multiple galaxies. Technology has advanced beyond imagination, and people now communicate through telepathic links facilitated by quantum neural networks. The world has evolved into a seamless blend of virtual and physical realities, with holographic displays and augmented reality being a part of everyday life.
Human civilization has established contact with extraterrestrial beings, forming alliances and sharing knowledge across the universe. The fabrication of space and time has become a reality, enabling time travel and instantaneous transportation between distant galaxies. This era represents a pinnacle of human achievement, where the possibilities are as vast as the cosmos itself.
In the Andromeda galaxy, humanity has encountered the ancient and powerful Annunaki, a species believed to have influenced early human civilizations. Through a shared pursuit of knowledge and technology, humans and Annunaki work together to explore the farthest reaches of the universe, unlocking the mysteries of existence and the secrets of space and time.
